Hey there!

It's me, Pooja, and it's nice to see you here.

Hi, I'm Pooja. Originally from India, now living in Berlin. I've learned this craft mostly by doing: starting in VFX, teaching myself UX, and later formalising that with a CUA certification from Human Factors International.

I'm a curious creative who likes exploring new ideas and possibilities, balanced with a grounded, practical side that likes figuring out what actually works and what it takes to get there.

As a Senior Product Designer with experience across mobile, enterprise, automotive and fintech, I bring both visual craft and structured UX thinking to every project I take on.

My path here wasn't traditional. Working in VFX taught me the value of visual craft, but over time I realised I wanted to shape not just how something looks, but how people actually use it. That realisation led me into UX and product design.

Through my work, I want to help build products that are clear, useful, and genuinely easy to live with, ones that hold up in the real world and not just in a mockup.

Outside of work, I'm just as curious, always planning the next trip or trying something new in the kitchen.

Pooja Kurade. Berlin, Germany
